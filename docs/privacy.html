鮮划算 · 隱私權政策

最後更新：2026 年 7 月 12 日

我們不收集你的個人資料。

不需要帳號

鮮划算不需註冊或登入，也不會向你索取姓名、電話、Email 或位置。

資料只留在你的裝置

你的追蹤清單與偏好設定只儲存在本機，不會上傳到任何伺服器。

行情內建，連線只為更新

行情本來就打包在 App 裡，完全離線也能用。唯一的連外行為，是下載一份我們預先整理好的公開行情檔（資料源自農業部資料開放平臺）；請求不夾帶任何可識別你的資訊。

沒有第三方追蹤

沒有廣告追蹤、沒有分析 SDK，不會側錄你的使用行為。

FreshKnow · Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

We don't collect your personal data.

No account needed

FreshKnow needs no sign-up or login, and never asks for your name, phone, email, or location.

Your data stays on your device

Your tracking lists and preferences are stored only on your device and are never uploaded.

Prices are built in; we only fetch updates

Prices ship inside the app and work fully offline. The only network activity is downloading a pre-built public price file (data from Taiwan's Ministry of Agriculture open-data platform)—no information that could identify you is ever sent.

No third-party tracking

No ad tracking and no analytics SDKs—we don't profile how you use the app.
